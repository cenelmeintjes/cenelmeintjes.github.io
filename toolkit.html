Communication & Messaging

Slack
Industry-standard team messaging platform
Best for: Organising client communication into channels, file sharing, and quick huddles. Pro from $7.25/user/month.
Free Plan
Microsoft Teams
Chat, calls, and file collaboration in one place
Best for: EAs within corporate Microsoft 365 environments — full Word, Excel, and Outlook integration. From $4/user/month.
Free Plan
Zoom
Gold standard for HD video meetings and screen sharing
Best for: Client calls, onboarding, and team meetings. Almost every client already uses Zoom. Pro from $12.49/month.
Free Plan
Google Meet
Free browser-based video calls — no downloads needed
Best for: Quick calls with Gmail or Google Workspace clients. Included in Workspace from $6/user/month.
Free Plan
Loom
Record and share short screen videos instead of long emails
Best for: Async video walkthroughs, tutorials, and status updates without scheduling a call. Business from $12.50/user/month.

Note-Taking & Voice-to-Text Conversion

Free · Built-In
📱 Google Voice Typing
Built into Android and Chrome — no app needed
Best for: Dictating emails and notes hands-free on Android or Chrome. Zero setup — activate via microphone icon on keyboard or Tools > Voice Typing in Google Docs.
Free · Built-In
📱 Gboard (Google Keyboard)
Google's smart keyboard with built-in voice input
Best for: Dictating messages and emails from your phone in any app — WhatsApp, Gmail, Slack. Tap the microphone and speak. Free on Android and iOS.
Free · Built-In
📱 Microsoft SwiftKey Keyboard
AI-powered keyboard with voice dictation and smart predictions
Best for: VAs in the Microsoft ecosystem — learns your writing style and includes voice-to-text across all apps. Free on Android and iOS.
Free Plan
📱 Otter.ai
Best-in-class for long-form speech and meeting transcription
Best for: Transcribing long meetings and voice notes in real time. Free plan: 300 min/month. Pro from $8.33/month. otter.ai
Free Plan
📱 Speechnotes
Clean, distraction-free voice-to-text notepad
Best for: Quick voice dictation into a notepad you can copy anywhere. No account needed — works instantly in Chrome.
Free PlanAI
🔥 Fireflies.ai
AI meeting recorder — transcribes, summarises, extracts action items
Best for: EAs attending meetings on behalf of executives — joins your call automatically, emails full transcript and action items. Pro from $10/user/month. fireflies.ai
Paid
✍️ MyScript
Converts handwritten notes on screen into typed text in real time
Best for: VAs and EAs who prefer writing by hand on a tablet — converts handwriting to digital text across 63 languages. myscript.com
Paid
✍️ Paperlike (formerly Nebo)
Premium handwriting-to-text app for iPad and tablet professionals
Best for: Remote professionals using an iPad with Apple Pencil — converts handwriting to text and exports to Word or PDF. paperlike.com
